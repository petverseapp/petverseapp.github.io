We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). This Privacy Policy explains what data PetVerse collects, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

To access any PetVerse features, you must create a user account. During sign-up we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Password (securely hashed; never stored in plain text)

We do not require sensitive personal data (e.g., home address, government IDs). Please refrain from sharing such information in AI chat or elsewhere in the App.

1.2 AI Chat Input & History

All text you send to PetVerse's AI chat is forwarded in real time to OpenAI's API to generate replies. Your chat history is stored in Cloud Firestore under your account so you can continue conversations across devices. We do not use your chat content for any purpose other than syncing your history and fetching AI responses.

Important: OpenAI processes your inputs on their servers under their API usage policies, which do not use API inputs to train their models by default. Do not include private, sensitive, or personally identifying information in your chats. For current details on OpenAI's data handling, see openai.com/policies/api-data-usage-policies.

1.3 Health & Usage Data

Every metric you log (water intake, litter-box activity, weight, energy level, medication/vaccination notes, etc.) is tied to your account and synced to our backend. We use this data solely to power and personalize your pet-care experience and to generate AI insights.

1.4 Account Sharing Data

PetVerse allows you to invite other users to share access to your pet profiles via invite codes. When account sharing is active:

  • The invited user's email address is collected to send the invitation (via SendGrid).
  • Both account holders can view and edit shared pet data, logs, and records.
  • The account owner controls access and can revoke sharing at any time via Settings > Account Sharing.
  • Each user remains individually responsible for their own activity under these Terms.
  • We do not merge or link separate user accounts; sharing is scoped to specific pet profiles only.

1.5 Device & Technical Information

We automatically collect non-personal technical details via Firebase Analytics (only if you opt in), including:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Unique device identifier and IP address
  • Timestamps and in-app events (e.g., which screens you visit, how often you use features)

To sync your data and queue actions when offline, PetVerse requires INTERNET and ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE permissions—so we can detect connectivity and retry failed requests.

Analytics Consent

On first launch we ask if you'd like to help improve PetVerse by sharing anonymous usage data. You can withdraw consent at any time via Settings > Privacy > Share Usage Analytics. Turning this off disables all new analytics data collection.

Retention

We retain analytics identifiers for up to 12 months. After 6 months, raw device IDs and debug logs are irreversibly de-identified or deleted.

1.6 Permissions & Sensor Data

  • Camera (CAMERA): Requested if you choose to take photos for pet avatars or health record attachments (e.g., medication labels or vet receipts). Photos are stored securely in Cloud Firestore/Storage and synced to your account.
  • Storage/Media (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Requested only if you choose to import or export photos (e.g., custom avatars or health record attachments). Any accessed files remain under your control unless you explicitly sync them to your account.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): We request this so we can remind you about logged events (e.g., medication schedules, weight checks).
  • Exact Alarms (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM & USE_EXACT_ALARM): Needed to fire medication, metric-check, and vet-visit reminders at precise times.
  • Battery Optimization (REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS): We may request to be excluded from battery optimization to ensure that time-sensitive reminders (like medications or vet visits) are delivered reliably even when the device is idle.
  • No other sensors: PetVerse does not request access to your microphone, contacts, location, or any other personal data—only the permissions listed above.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 To Provide and Maintain the Service (Mandatory)

Data involved: Account Information, AI Chat Inputs, Chat History, Health & Usage Data, Account Sharing Data, Device & Technical Data.

What we do:

  • Forward your AI chat inputs to OpenAI in real time and return replies.
  • Store and sync your chat history and pet metrics in Firestore so PetVerse works across devices.
  • Process account sharing invitations and maintain shared access to designated pet profiles.
  • Use device identifiers and in-app event logs to ensure features load and run correctly.

2.2 To Improve the App (Optional Analytics)

Data involved: Anonymous, aggregated Firebase Analytics (only if you opt in).

  • Identify and fix bugs, performance bottlenecks, and crash patterns.
  • Measure feature usage to guide UI/UX refinements and new feature development.
  • Validate updates against real-world usage trends.

Retention: Analytics identifiers retained up to 12 months; raw identifiers purged after 6 months.

2.3 To Communicate with You

2.3.1 Service & Support Notifications (Mandatory)

We use your email to respond to support requests and send critical account or policy notices.

2.3.2 Marketing & Newsletters (Opt-In Only)

We send promotional or newsletter emails only if you explicitly opt in via an in-app toggle.

2.3.3 Anti-Spam Compliance (CASL)

  • We will only send commercial emails if you have expressly opted in.
  • All commercial emails will clearly identify PetVerse as the sender and include a valid mailing address and a no-cost unsubscribe mechanism.
  • We will honour unsubscribe requests within 10 business days.
  • We do not rely on implied consent for any marketing emails.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We share your information only with trusted providers who assist us in operating the App:

  • Firebase / Google Cloud Platform: Hosts all backend data (Firestore, Authentication, Analytics, Crashlytics). Governed by Google's Data Processing Terms.
  • OpenAI: Processes AI chat inputs to generate responses. Governed by OpenAI's API usage policies.
  • SendGrid (Twilio): Sends account-sharing invitation emails.

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only as directed by us.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to: comply with legal processes (court orders, subpoenas); protect the rights, property, or safety of PetVerse, our users, or the public; or prevent fraud, security breaches, or other illegal activity.

3.3 Business Transfers

If PetVerse is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you via email and/or a prominent in-app notice before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

3.4 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. We do not store or process your credit card information; all payments are handled securely by Google Play.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

4.1 Active Accounts

  • Account and health data retained as long as your account is active.
  • Chat history retained until you delete it (individual chat deletion available in-app; bulk deletion available via account deletion).
  • Analytics data retained up to 12 months.

4.2 Deleted Accounts

  • Account deletion removes all personal data within 30 days.
  • Some aggregated, anonymized analytics may persist for statistical purposes.
  • Legal or fraud-prevention records may be retained longer as required by law.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Correction

You can view and update your account information and pet data directly in the App under Settings.

5.2 Data Export

Request a copy of your data by emailing cozycatapp@gmail.com with subject line "PetVerse Data Export Request." We will provide it in a portable format within 30 days.

5.3 Account Deletion

Delete your account via Settings > Account > Delete Account. This removes all your data within 30 days.

5.4 Opt-Out of Analytics

Disable analytics collection via Settings > Privacy > Share Usage Analytics.

5.5 Marketing Communications

Unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link in any promotional email, or update your preferences in Settings.

5.6 Revoke Account Sharing

Remove any shared user from your account at any time via Settings > Account Sharing. Revocation takes effect immediately.

6. Children's Privacy

PetVerse is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us.

7. International Data Transfers

PetVerse is operated from Canada. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to Canada and to other countries where our service providers (Firebase/Google, OpenAI, SendGrid) operate. These countries may have different data protection laws than your country of residence. We rely on standard contractual clauses and provider certifications to ensure adequate protection of your data in these transfers.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Data encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Data encrypted at rest (Firebase encryption)
  • Secure authentication (Firebase Auth)
  • Periodic security reviews and dependency updates

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and we encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via in-app notification and/or email to your registered address. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
